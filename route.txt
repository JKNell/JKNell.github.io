Faux Grid Master starting route (Further Optimized)
Version 4.3

XP tiles: Middle Row/Column, Barrows (def from DS2 exp rewards), Lunar chest (Jad unlocks), Arraxor (92 Slayer) 
XP Lamps: 500 total (25k Hunter), 1500 total (100K)
Combat mastery: Scurrius, Bryo, 4x row completions

Gatherer: Thieving/Slayer/Agility
Craftsman: Construction/Cooking/Herblore
Combatant: Strength/Ranged/Prayer

Power miner, Teleports, Basic tile sequence
- Complete tutorial
- Choose Power miner
- Count check to stronghold, equip dramen staff, shield
- Pick up 4-6 Meat (hop worlds) in long hall to the north
- Stronghold all 4 floors to Fancy boots, claim Fairy's flight (If you're doing this after the initial release, pick up 2-3 world hops of Cooked meat in the house to the north before going into the stronghold)
- Take vine upstairs
- Mine Tin ore for 15 mining
- Run to Varrock east bank
- Fairy's flight Gnome Stronghold spirit tree. Throw 1 gnomeball 
- Fairy's flight Tool leprechaun to Aldarin
- Run South to Mistrock bar, pick up Steamforge brew and drink. 
- Fairy's flight to Grand Exchange, claim Sage's greaves and Banker's briefcase
- Banker's briefcase to Rionasta
- Run south to the Scorched earth from Jungle Potion, search 3 for herbs. Clean herbs (https://i.imgur.com/XL5m89K.png)
- Banker's briefcase to Varrock east
- Claim Friendly Forager

Combat start, Gatherer sequence
- Deposit all, withdraw coins, air runes, mind runes, Power miner axe, mushroom, briefcase, forager, food
- Buy 2 mind pack, 50 chaos runes, 2 water packs, Air staff, HAMMER at general store, pink skirt at clothing shop
- Fairy's flight tele to Kastori North
- Run north, hop worlds to a world where Gemstone crab isn't at the Northern location (no Iron ore). Train mining at Iron until Crab burrows. 
- Unlock gatherer relic. Use on Thieving, Agility, Slayer 
- Fairy's flight to DIS
- Kill Lesser Demon downstairs with WATER SPELLS (single combat). Safespot through fence
- Banker's briefcase draynor bank
- Buy 15 wines from Fortunado
- Start Vampire slayer at Morgan
- Grab garlic upstairs from cupboard
- Banker's briefcase to Varrock east
- Run to Blue Moon Inn
- Buy a beer
- Speak to Harlow to get stake
- Fairy's flight Leprechaun to Draynor manor patch
- Kill count draynor with magic
- Unlock Vamp Slayer reward

Golden God, Gold Ore, First Horizontal Column Reward Sequence

- Banker's briefcase to Varrock east, thieve Tea stall to 25 thieving (same xp as cake)
- Banker's briefcase to Hosidius, run east to fruit stalls (in house, don't use market)
- Thieve fruit stall once, unlock dodgy deals
- Continue Fruit stall to level 36. Keep 1 Jangerberry
- Fairy's Flight Leprechaun to Aldarin, go upstairs in house to the northwest. Thieve from chest (spam click) to 45
- Fairy's flight Leprechaun to Varrock tree patch
- Pickpocket Guards upstairs in castle for best AoE loot to level 50 
- Banker's Briefcase to Civitas illa fortis west
- Steal valuables in Wealthy citizen house to unlock Golden God
- Banker's briefcase last destination 
- Varlamore Blacksmith, buy and alch mith/addy platebodies until 200k cash. Pickpocket guard outside while you alch. Spend full cash stack on addy platebodies after 200k. 
- Fairy's flight Leprechaun to Locus Oasis
- Alch gold leaf at Stonemason for Production Prodigy relic
- Claim MIDDLE HORIZONTAL COLUMN REWARD (+200% Exp)
- Train mining at Iron to the south while alching Gold Leaves (buy no more than 5 at once, more gets too expensive. Never shift click buy 50)
- Mine until level 37, special attack with pickaxe, mine gold ore. Buy more gold leaves every time you run out of leaf stack.
- Goal for 99 con: ~5m gp+

Woodcutting, Combatant, Crafting sequence
- Bankers briefcase to Al Kharid
- Claim lumberjack axe at bank. Set axe option to "fletching"
- Fairy's flight to tele to DJP
- Cut trees, oak trees, willow trees to 35 WC. Spam click tree to continue alching
- Fairy's flight Leprechaun to Locus Oasis
- Set lumberjack axe to Firemaking, cut teaks to 42
- Fairy ring to CIR. Spec with Lumberjack axe, cut maple tree
- Banker's briefcase to Al Kharid, claim Harpoon
- Fairy's flight to Port Sarim leprechaun
- Go to Betty, Buy eye of newt, as many chaos/death/elemental runes as you want (I'm buying 5k+ each).
- Run to Port Sarim fishing shop, buy a few bait packs, 20k+ feathers
- Buy 10 buckets of sand/soda ash at charter for crafting start
- Banker's briefcase to Corsair cove
- Deposit Sand/Soda ash
- Kill Ogress warrior with best earth spell in safespot. Defensive cast.
- Banker's Briefcase to Edgeville
- Claim combatant, Choose Ranged/Strength/Prayer 
- Deposit all, withdraw coins, banker's briefcase,  9 buckets of sand/soda ash. make molten glass for 8+ crafting
- Banker's briefcase to mage bank. Buy 1k+ Law runes, buy 50 cosmic runes
- Banker's briefcase to Aldarin
- Bank all, withdraw coins, buy chisel at gen store to northeast
- Train crafting at gem shop. Buy Sapphires at 8, Emeralds at 15 (Emeralds are faster than rubies for crafting xp/hr because of purchase speed. I'll stop at 50 for now. Buy 1 ruby for str ammy.)

Tempoross sequence for Craftsman
- Banker's briefcase to Al Kharid
- Withdraw Harpoon, Fishing bait, Feathers
- Fish Shrimp to the south of Al Kharid bank to level 5
- Bait fish at shrimp spot to level 20
- Fairy's flight Leprechaun to Lumbridge, fish trout/salmon to 35
- Banker's briefcase to Unkah
- Complete 1 Tempoross. Bring Echo harpoon, grab rope once you're on boat.  
- Unlock Craftsman, I'm choosing Construction/Herblore/Cooking

Cooking, Corrupted Shark
- Banker's briefcase to Piscarillius
- Buy 50 sardine, cod, tuna, lobster, 600+ swordfish, 1+ raw shark
- Banker's briefcase to Shayzien 
- Run to clothing shop (east of south shayzien bank), buy chef's hat
- Banker's briefcase to Rogues den
- Cook food at fire for 68 cooking
- Cook 1 shark
- Claim Corrupted shark

Prayer -> Ranged gear -> Bryophyta sequence
- Banker's briefcase Varrock east
- Buy studded body from Horvik in Varrock 
- Fairy's flight leprechaun to Champion's guild get green dhide chaps/vambs, rune platelegs and chain
- Banker's briefcase Varrock west
- Deposit all, withdraw hammer, steel bars
- Smith 2 inventories of Steel knives with steel bars, rest on dart tips
- Banker's briefcase Ourania
- Train pray at altar upstairs to 60 ranged (~67 prayer + Combatant split). Use coins on altar.
- Kill Scurrius (steel knives). Use Corrupted shark (unlimited food). Train HP to 40+ first if you expect to struggle with this. (use private instance is 500hp vs public's 1500) 
- Kill moss giants for mossy key (Wildy has better drop rates, varrock sewers, karamja, kourend catacombs, or giants den otherwise)
- Kill bryophyta (bring an axe) for +1 Combat mastery tile, unlock 9x exp

9x exp rate unlocked

Hunter, DS2 unlock for 70 Defence
- Use 25k Exp lamp in Hunter (boosts to 58, skips early slow levels)
- Catch grey chins in Kourend woodland until 63
- Catch red chins in Feldip hills or Varlamore until 73
- Banker's briefcase to Hunter's guild
- Go downstairs, pick up Trapper's tipple
- Run south of hunter's guild. Drink tipple, catch sunlight moth 
- Unlock DS2 from moth reward
- Banker's briefcase to Myth guild
- Talk to Ellen on ground floor for 4x 25k combat rewards (Use 3x in Defence (level 69), 1x in Attack)
- Chin longrange at Dark warriors castle to finish 70 Def

Barrows sequence
- Banker's briefcase to Yanille
- Buy full mystic in Mage guild (not boots)
- Complete Witch's house for HP exp (cheese in Draynor, gloves at Varrock clothes shop)
- Banker's briefcase to Arceuus, run east to gen store. Buy blood/soul runes.
- Banker's briefcase to Burg de rott
- Complete barrows until 2x items (Air spells on defensive to finish 70 def)
- Wear the first barrows item you get to complete task (loot chest while wearing barrows item)
- Claim +200% experience reward

Fruit tree sequence
- Check bank for nests, open for low level fruit tree (apple, banana)
- If none, kill mammoths southeast of Ferox Enclave for Apple or Banana seed
- Buy Bagged yew trees from Fally park Garden supplier
- Plant in Garden in PoH for fruit tree farming level
- Plant at any fruit patch (~3.5 hours to grow)

Lizard Shaman tile tips: Use a Maple Shortbow + Adamant Arrows, or Obby Rings. Stand at max distance, run 3 tiles away from poison splat to avoid damage. 

Torstol tip: Gather with Friendly Forager after Construction sequence (high Herb level helps). Kill Monk of Zamorak near Ourania altar for Zammy Top/Bottom. Make potion at SOUTHEASTERN VARROCK ALTAR (near mage shop)

Twisted Bow tips: 0-150 Invocation ToA for Megarare (nearly guaranteed Thread of Elidinis/gem, which unlocks the tile)

Jad kill unlocks Perilous Moons. Can kill 1 moon and loot chest to complete tile.

Huey tips: Try to get a small 3-5 man team. Masses are bad for loot.
